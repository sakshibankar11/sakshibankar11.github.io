About Auroskills

Auroskill is an Ed-tech startup which provides different certification courses and placement calls to candidates. Auroskill is committed to facilitating real-world opportunities for its candidates. Through strategic partnerships with leading companies and organizations, they connect graduates with promising career prospects, helping them embark on fulfilling professional journeys.

Logo Design

Auro means gold in Greek, so the color scheme used in the logo is a golden hue to reflect upon this ideal while also matching it with a black-and-white backdrop that gives the project an overall professional and clean look. The graduation cap represents our main target audience of recent graduates who are unable to properly enter the workforce after completing their schooling and the image of a person shows how they'll achieve success and gain skills through this platform.
