About Me

Designer & Problem Solver

Hi, I'm Sakshi!

I'm currently pursuing my Master's degree in Informatics, specializing in Human-Centered Design. My journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Business and Computer Applications, where I discovered my interest in learning how technology can be used to transform and empower businesses.

While learning about business, I realized the importance of customer satisfaction, and my learning interest started shifting more towards understanding how humans interact with technology so that it can be leveraged effectively to empower businesses.

During my previous employment as a UI/UX Developer, I have designed solutions that enhance not only the customer experience but also the overall business process, ensuring more profits for the business.

I have gained a deep understanding of how Business, Technology, and User-Centered Design work together during an entire product development lifecycle. In my free time, I enjoy creating digital art and exploring new places and cultures.
