App Revamp for BPMS- Filta

  • My Role : UI/UX Designer
  • Duration : 3 weeks | Kitchen services management software (mobile app)
  • Tools : Figma, Illustrator
  • Methods : User Research, UI Redesign, High fidelity prototypes.

About Filta App

Filta, an app that manages kitchen equipment cleaning services for restaurants and other food establishments, aimed to improve the user experience for its customers. With this goal in mind, I revamped the app's user interface (UI) to make it more visually appealing and intuitive. Through a combination of field research, usability testing on prototypes, I changed UI elements in size and layouts to overcome the UX problems faced by users and resulted in a more user-friendly app.

UI Revamp for Kitchen Services Management App - Redesigned the interface for a kitchen service app by addressing usability issues via layout restructuring and visual hierarchy improvements. Conducted user research, usability testing, and workflow optimization, resulting in enhanced task efficiency and user satisfaction. Company's/Client's website- https://gofilta.com/, I revamped this filta symphony app which is live on App store-https://apps.apple.com/us/app/filta-symphony/id1494289896 The App facilitate THE FILTA GROUP, INC. Technicians and Franchisees to track their jobs and time onsite and to enter service information. I gathered feedback from the field workers who are Maintenance Technicians and Service Technicians, from observation i realized one the problem they had with the existing application is they wear golves during their work and it was difficult to tap/click on the screen and make entries in the app simultaneously while working, so i made sure the Ui was designed in a way where the click areas were bigger with enough spacing between it to ensure the workers can stil use the interface with gloves on. i also made some other changes to improve the color contrast, follow accessibility standards and improve workflows and copywriting to avoid confusion and designed this one of it's kind complex application, you cna see the before and after interface below.
