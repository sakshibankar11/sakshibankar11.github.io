Geo-Fencing to Automate

  • My Role : UI/UX Designer
  • Duration : 7 weeks | Resort Cleaning management software (mobile app)
  • Tools : Figma, Illustrator
  • Methods : Domain Research, brainstorming and Ideation, High fidelity prototypes.

About resort cleaning management software

Maintaining a clean and well-organized resort is crucial for providing guests with a comfortable and enjoyable experience. The resort cleaning management software is a tool that is used to schedule and manage the cleaning staff at a resort, but its current state of user interface and functionality may not be meeting the expectations of the staff and management. With the aim to enhance the usability and effectiveness of the cleaning management software, this case study will take a deep dive into how a user-centered design approach was employed to revamp the software by improving its UI and incorporating geo-fencing for automatic staff login and logout. The ultimate goal of this project is to develop a more efficient and user-friendly software that streamlines resort cleaning operations and elevates the experience for both staff and management.

Proposed Geo-fencing solution

In at attempt to improve the staff login and logout system, I researched, analyzed and proposed following solution for implementing geo-fencing technology to improve staff login and logout accuracy in this resort cleaning management software. it would automate the logging process, provide real-time tracking, and improve efficiency and user-friendliness of the software. The proposed solution has not been implemented yet but is expected to bring significant benefits.

Background on Geo-Fencing
  • Geo-fencing is a location-based technology that uses GPS, RFID, or WiFi to create virtual boundaries around a physical location.
  • Virtual boundaries (geo-fences) created around service locations at the resort, system detects when staff enters or exits and automatically logs the time.
  • Eliminates errors and inaccuracies that can occur with manual logging, and also provides real-time tracking of staff location and status.
Implementation in Resort Cleaning Management
  • Can be used to automatically register staff login and logout times based on their location
  • Common uses include mobile marketing and targeted advertising, but it can also be used in other industries such as retail, transportation, and facility management.
Steps for Implementation
  • Identify service locations at the resort (guest rooms, common areas, pool areas)
  • Create virtual boundaries (geo-fences) around these locations using GPS coordinates.
  • Install app on staff smartphones that can detect when they are inside or outside of the geo-fences.
  • Communicate with cleaning management software, easy to use and understand.
  • Train staff on how to use the app and ensure smartphones are properly configured to detect location.
Benefits
  • Improves accuracy of staff login and logout times.
  • Automates logging process.
  • Provides real-time tracking of staff location and status.
  • More efficient and user-friendly software.
  • Helps resort management team to better manage cleaning staff and operations.

Revamping UI

I also redesigned the existing resort cleaning management software's user interface to make it more visually appealing, user-friendly and efficient by incorporating modifications to the structure and layout, clear typography, visual elements and responsive design. This redesign improved the overall usability and effectiveness of the software.
