Designing for Logistics

  • My Role : UI/UX Designer
  • Duration : 4 weeks | Logistic Company (desktop app)
  • Tools : Figma, Illustrator
  • Methods : Domain Research, Wireframing
Problem Statement

Challenges to design logistic system

To design a desktop application for managing operations of a logistics company that specializes in order processing and services such as warehousing, picking, packing, and shipping. The company receives, processes, and stores inventory, and the application will aid in this process by utilizing a database that contains information on various internal operations. Additionally, it will provide real-time communication through notifications and alerts, keeping all live data at hand. The main challenges are-

  • To design an interface that enables easy access to the heavy databases used by the logistics company. Heavy databases can often be overwhelming and difficult to navigate, making it challenging for employees to efficiently access the information they need. Our goal is to create an interface that simplifies the process of finding, sorting and using the data stored in the databases.
  • To design of a user specific notification module. Effective communication is crucial for the smooth running of the logistics company's operations. The notification module will be designed to send and receive alerts in real-time, ensuring that all employees are informed of any updates or changes to the company's operations.

Domain Research

  • Industry Overview: Technological advancements have played a significant role in shaping the logistics industry by improving efficiency, reducing costs and increasing transparency. The integration of technology has become an essential part of the logistics process, from warehouse management to last-mile delivery.
  • Technological Advancements: Key technological advancements in logistics include:
    • Autonomous vehicles and drones for last-mile delivery
    • Internet of Things (IoT) for real-time tracking and monitoring of cargo
    • Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning for optimization of routes and forecasting demand
    • Blockchain for secure and transparent tracking of goods and transactions
    • Robotic process automation (RPA) for warehouse management and inventory tracking
  • Market Adoption: The adoption of these technologies varies across different regions and segments of the logistics industry. For example, the use of autonomous vehicles and drones is more prevalent in developed regions such as North America and Europe. On the other hand, the use of IoT and blockchain is expected to grow significantly in the Asia-Pacific region in the coming years.
  • Challenges: The integration of technology in logistics is not without its challenges. One of the main challenges is the high cost of implementation and maintenance, which can be a barrier for small and medium-sized logistics companies. Additionally, there is a lack of standardization and interoperability among different technologies, which can cause difficulties in integrating them into existing logistics systems.
  • Opportunities: The increasing adoption of technology in logistics presents a significant opportunity for companies to improve efficiency, reduce costs and increase transparency. Additionally, the growing trend towards e-commerce and online shopping has increased the demand for last-mile delivery services, providing an opportunity for logistics companies to grow their business.
  • Conclusion: The incorporation of technology in logistics is crucial for companies to stay competitive in the market. Companies that invest in technological advancements will be better positioned to handle the changing demands of the logistics industry and customers.

Ideate

Based on an analysis of the logistics industry and the various technological advancements that have been integrated into the logistics process, the following solutions were proposed in collaboration with the business analyst and technical lead of this project.

  • Implementing a data visualization system utilizing data tables to effectively analyze and manage large volumes of data.
  • Integrating robust filtering and sorting capabilities within the data tables to enhance data accessibility and organization.
  • Developing a notification module that enables efficient bulk messaging through various APIs.
  • Implementing a template-based notification module to allow for swift and streamlined communication to multiple users.
