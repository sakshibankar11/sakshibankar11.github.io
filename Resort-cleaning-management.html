Problem Statement

Manual clock-in/out processes created time tracking discrepancies and poor user experience. Staff struggled with an outdated interface while management lost visibility into operations.

The Solution

QR code integration + complete UI overhaul eliminated time discrepancies while streamlining the entire workflow. Result: industry-leading cleaning management platform.

Key Pain Points Identified

  • Inaccurate Time Tracking: Manual login/logout processes led to errors and potential time theft
  • Poor User Interface: Outdated design hindered staff productivity and adoption
  • Operational Inefficiency: Staff spent excessive time on administrative tasks rather than cleaning
  • Real-time Tracking Issues: Management lacked visibility into staff location and task status

About Resort Cleaning Management Software

Maintaining a clean and well-organized resort is crucial for providing guests with a comfortable and enjoyable experience. The resort cleaning management software is a tool that is used to schedule and manage the cleaning staff at a resort, but its current state of user interface and functionality may not be meeting the expectations of the staff and management. With the aim to enhance the usability and effectiveness of the cleaning management software, this case study will take a deep dive into how a user-centered design approach was employed to revamp the software by improving its UI and incorporating geo-fencing for automatic staff login and logout.

Key Implementation Benefits

  • Eliminated Time Discrepancies: QR scanning ensures staff are physically present at work locations
  • Streamlined User Experience: One-tap check-in/out process integrated seamlessly into daily workflow
  • Enhanced Accountability: Real-time location verification prevents time theft and improves transparency
  • Reduced Administrative Burden: Automated tracking eliminates manual time sheet reconciliation
