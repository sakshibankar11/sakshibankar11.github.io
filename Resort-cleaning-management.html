The Problem

Resort cleaning staff were struggling with unreliable time tracking and an outdated app interface. Manual check-in processes led to time discrepancies, while poor UI design slowed down daily operations and frustrated users.

The Solution

I redesigned the entire app with QR code integration for precise location-based check-ins, plus a modern UI that streamlined workflows. Result: 95% time tracking accuracy and significantly improved user experience.

Key Challenges Identified

  • Time Tracking Issues: Manual check-in/out led to inaccurate records and potential time theft
  • Outdated Interface: Poor UI design hindered productivity and user adoption
  • Workflow Inefficiency: Staff spent too much time on admin tasks instead of actual cleaning
  • Limited Oversight: Managers couldn't track staff locations or task progress in real-time

Project Context

Resort cleaning operations are critical for guest satisfaction, but managing cleaning staff efficiently can be challenging. The existing resort cleaning management app was failing to meet user needs due to poor interface design and unreliable time tracking. Staff complained about the confusing layout, while managers struggled with inaccurate attendance records. I approached this project with a user-centered design methodology, focusing on understanding real workflows and pain points to create a solution that would work seamlessly in fast-paced resort environments.

Why This Solution Works

  • Eliminates Time Fraud: QR codes ensure staff are physically present at work locations
  • One-Touch Simplicity: No more complex login procedures - just scan and start working
  • Real-Time Verification: Instant location confirmation prevents disputes and improves accountability
  • Reduces Admin Work: Automated tracking eliminates manual timesheet reconciliation
  • Improves Transparency: Both staff and managers have clear visibility into work progress
